Each crystal is worth a secret value. Select the crystals to increase your total amount. If you are able to make your total equal the GOAL value exactly --- you win! But if your total goes above the GOAL value --- you lose.

After each round is completed, a new goal number is set and new values are assigned to each jewel.

You may adjust the difficulty by changing the number of jewels that are available.
